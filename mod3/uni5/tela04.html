E.; SAVICKAS, M.; & SUPER, C. The Life-span, life-space approach to careers. In: BROWN, D. & BROOKS, L. (Eds)., Carreer choice and development (pp. 121-178). San Francisco, CA: Jossey-Bass, 1996).
